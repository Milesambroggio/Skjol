Life in Avon

Beaver Creek Village

World-class skiing and dining just five minutes away. An alpine village known for immaculate grooming, fine dining, and a refined mountain experience.

Vail Village

The iconic heart of the valley — cobblestone streets, boutique shopping, galleries, and après-ski culture eight minutes from your door.

Eagle River

Gold Medal fly fishing, whitewater rafting, and scenic riverside trails winding through the valley floor.

Nottingham Park

Avon's lakeside gathering place — summer concerts, farmers markets, paddleboarding, and year-round recreation steps from Skjol.

Year-Round Living

Not Just a Ski Home. A Mountain Life.

Hagalaz

Winter

Deep powder days at two world-class resorts, just minutes from your door. End the day with fireside dining and legendary mountain culture.

Berkana

Spring

Chase late-season turns in the morning, then watch wildflowers bloom across the valley. Spring runoff brings world-class rafting to your backyard.

Sowilo

Summer

Hike alpine trails, ride singletrack, tee off with mountain views, or cast a fly on Gold Medal waters. Long days and cool mountain nights.

Jera

Autumn

Golden aspens paint the valley in amber and fire. Harvest festivals, uncrowded trails, and crisp mountain air make fall the locals' secret season.
