The Residences

Crafted for how you actually live

Design Philosophy

Every residence at Skjol draws from the Scandinavian tradition of purposeful beauty -- clean lines that calm the eye, warm materials that invite touch, and floor plans that flex around the way real life unfolds. Light pours through floor-to-ceiling glass. European oak grounds each room. Stone and timber echo the landscape just beyond the balcony. These are not spec units dressed up for a brochure; they are homes shaped by how people actually cook, gather, rest, and recharge in the mountains.
