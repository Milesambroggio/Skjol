The Amenities

Where the lodge meets modern living

Life at Skjol

Every shared space at Skjol is designed around the Scandinavian concept of hygge -- that untranslatable feeling of warmth, connection, and togetherness. From the firelit lounge to the rooftop terrace, these are places where neighbors become friends and every season feels like an invitation to gather.

Wellness

Pool & Spa

Restore body and mind in spaces inspired by Nordic bathing traditions and alpine vitality. A resort-style pool with waterslide, multiple spas and hot tubs, and fire pits create an outdoor oasis in every season.

  • Resort-style pool with waterslide
  • Multiple spas and hot tubs
  • Finnish sauna
  • Outdoor fire pits and grilling areas
Fitness

Fitness & Movement

A state-of-the-art fitness center designed for everything from morning cardio to serious strength training. Complement your workout in the dedicated yoga studio or recover in the spa after a long day on the mountain.

  • State-of-the-art fitness center
  • Yoga and movement studio
  • Indoor and outdoor pickleball
Recreation

Social & Entertainment

Community is built in shared moments. The golf and multi-sport simulator brings year-round play indoors, while the private club room, cafe, and lounge spaces give neighbors a reason to linger.

  • Golf and multi-sport simulator
  • Private club room
  • Cafe and coffee bar
  • Owner's lounge with fireside seating
Convenience

Ski & Gear Storage

Every practical detail has been considered so you can spend less time managing and more time living. Warm boots waiting in your ski locker, gear dried and ready — Skjol removes the friction from mountain life.

  • Private ski lockers with boot warmers
  • Heated underground parking
  • Bike storage and workshop
  • EV charging stations
Services

Transportation & Concierge

A dedicated ski shuttle runs directly to Vail and Beaver Creek, so the mountain is always minutes away. On-site hospitality and property management handle the details, whether you're in residence or away.

  • Dedicated ski shuttle to Vail & Beaver Creek
  • On-site hospitality and front desk
  • Property management services
  • Concierge services
